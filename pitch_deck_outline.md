## Slide 2: The Problem & The Gap
**Title:** The Blind Spot in Conservation

**Core Message:** Kenya's forests are under constant threat, but current monitoring systems are either too slow, too expensive, or fail to measure the full cycle of conservation.

**Problem Statement:**
- **Illegal Logging/Fire:** Manual patrols and satellite imagery lead to slow response times
- **Reforestation Failure:** We track planting but not survival - the true measure of impact
- **Network Access:** GSM/4G only works in limited areas, leaving critical forests unprotected

**Supporting Data:**
- Kenya loses over 200,000 acres of forest annually
- 60% of reforestation efforts fail due to lack of monitoring
- 70% of critical forest areas lack reliable network coverage

## Slide 3: The Solution: Full-Cycle Impact
**Title:** Acoustic Guardian: The Full-Cycle Forest Intelligence System

**Core Message:** We provide a two-pronged, data-driven approach that measures both the prevention of loss and the measurement of gain.

**The Digital Hummingbird Sensor Node (RPi + Camera + Mic + LoRa):**
1. **Threat Prevention:** Real-time acoustic AI detects chainsaws and fire, triggering instant alerts
2. **Growth Measurement:** Visual AI tracks the Tree Survival Rate and health of planted saplings

**The Digital Twin Dashboard (Grafana):**
- Interactive M&E Dashboard: Visualizes real-time threats, "Time Safe" metrics, and Tree Survival Rates
- AI-Powered Prediction: Uses historical data to predict high-risk zones for proactive deployment

**Key Innovation:** Hybrid LoRa/GSM communication solves the network problem
